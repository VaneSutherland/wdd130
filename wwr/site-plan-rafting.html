Overview

Purpose

Deliver a memorable experience for everyone who loves being in touch with nature but also enjoys a thrilling adventure. Our focus is on ensuring the highest safety standards, with well-trained and experienced professionals dedicated to providing excellent service. Whether you're a seasoned outdoor enthusiast or new to the world of rafting, we aim to create unforgettable journeys that combine the beauty of nature with the excitement of adventure

Audience

Who are the target customers? The ideal target audience for this rafting experience is broad and diverse. It encompasses adventure enthusiasts seeking exhilarating outdoor activities like rafting and kayaking, as well as nature lovers who enjoy exploring beautiful landscapes and spending time in the great outdoors. Families and friend groups looking for unique experiences to share, as well as tourists visiting the area

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? On the website, we'll have the necessary information to schedule times, view routes, and see photographs. Additionally, it will include experiences shared by other people and their feedback on the service.

How will my audience usually access my site? Access will generally be via mobile phone because people can connect easily from anywhere, but they can also access it from their laptop
